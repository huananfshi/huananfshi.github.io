Hi, I'm Huanan Shi.

I am a PhD candidate within the Molecular Physiology and Biophysics program at Baylor College of Medicine. My dissertation work in Dr. David Durgan's lab focuses on using multi-omic approaches and machine-learning algorithms to process biological information. I apply this approach to understand the physiological relevance of gut microbiota and its affects on blood pressure in hypertensive rat models.
